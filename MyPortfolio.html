Biography

Steven Paul Jobs(February 24, 2011- October 05, 2011) was an american entepreneur, industrial designer, business magnate, media proprieter and investor. He was the co-founder,chairman and CEO of Apple; the chairman and ,majority shareholder of Pixer; A member of Walt Disney Company's board of director following its aquisition of Pixer and the founder, chairman, and CEO of Next. He is widely recognized as prioneer of the personal computer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s, along with his early business partner and fellow Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak.
